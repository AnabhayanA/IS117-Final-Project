## Presentation Tips

### Before Presenting:
- [ ] Practice multiple times (aim for exactly 5 minutes)
- [ ] Test all links and interactive elements
- [ ] Prepare for technical difficulties (have PDF backup)
- [ ] Review the rubric and ensure all requirements are met
- [ ] Get feedback from peers or mentors

### During Presentation:
- [ ] Speak clearly and at a measured pace
- [ ] Make eye contact with the audience
- [ ] Use presenter notes but don't read verbatim
- [ ] Show enthusiasm and confidence
- [ ] Stick to the time limit (5 minutes)

### Visual Design Guidelines:
- Use Canva, Google Slides, or PowerPoint
- Maintain consistent color scheme and fonts
- Avoid bullet-heavy slides‚Äîuse visuals
- Ensure text is large enough to read from back of room
- High contrast for readability
- Professional, clean design
